## What 10–30 Inches Means on the Ground

- **10–15 in:** Post-holing likely on warm afternoons. Microspikes recommended. Passable but tiring.
- **15–22 in:** Consistent postholing, icy crust in morning. Microspikes essential; ice axe strongly recommended on any slope.
- **22–30 in:** Deep consolidated snowpack. Steep terrain will be icy. **Ice axe + crampons required** — a slip could be fatal on exposed slopes.

The Chikamin and Deception zones (25–30 in) fall firmly in the third category. These are not casual microspike terrain.
