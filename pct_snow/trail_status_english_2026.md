## Notes and Limitations

- Projections use real SNOTEL sensor data through **May 7, 2026** (53 days), then a weighted average of 2024 (35%) and 2025 (65%) for future dates
- The 16-day warm forecast (up to 21°C) is **not** incorporated into the model projections — actual clearance dates for July-range sections may come **earlier** than shown
- Model cannot account for: wind drifting, aspect (south-facing slopes melt 2–4 weeks earlier), or forest canopy shade
- Snow-free threshold: depth < 6 inches for 5 consecutive days
